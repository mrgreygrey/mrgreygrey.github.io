Strings man, how do they work?

Ahh, the venerable string. Whethe you are using an object- oriented language, procedural or functional you will need to learn about strings. They are ubiquitous and often critical in the reading and creation of data.

A string is at it's most basic just a string of characters chained together. The exciting part is how they can be manipulated. Here are a couple of common string methods and what they do.

method purpose
ToLower() makes all characters in the string lowercase
ToUpper() makes all characters in the string uppercase
Split(char[]) Splits the strings into smaller strings based on what character is in the parameters

Concatenation

Concatenation is the combining of two strings together. It sounds tricky but the good news it is easy peasy. To concatenate strings all you need to do is put a "+" sign in between them. like if I were to write:

"I'm" + " awesome"

and print it out, it would print "I'm awesome".
