What is the MVVM?

MVVW stands for the "Model View Viewmodel". It is a design practice used to ensure that the user never directly manipulates the database or the "Model". Instead the user only directly interacts with the "View" which then sends requests through the "Viewmodel" which in turn access the database to read, write or delete data as needed.

This protects the data, whether its from bad actors trying to maliciously manipulate your databases or an employee who is not supposed to have access to certain records. The viewmodel also allows us to determine how the data is presented to the view. Whether we want it in tables or we want to have it interpreted in graphs the viewmodel contains all of our business logic to get it done.
